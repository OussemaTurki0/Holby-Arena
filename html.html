About HolbyArena

Developed by Oussema Turki, HolbyArena is inspired by fast-paced shooters like Unreal Tournament. Built in Unreal Engine with support from Blender and Photoshop, this game offers high-stakes gameplay with strategic AI challenges.

In HolbyArena, players navigate through diverse and visually throw the holberton main building.

The advanced AI not only adapts to player strategies but also collaborates with environmental elements to create unpredictable combat scenarios.

HolbyArena also focuses on community engagement, offering regular updates and events, allowing players to compete for rewards and recognition. With a dedicated community, the game promotes teamwork and competitive spirit.
